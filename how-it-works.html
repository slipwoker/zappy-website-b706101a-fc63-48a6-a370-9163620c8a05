How Remote Online Notarization Works

A streamlined, fully digital process designed for U.S. citizens located anywhere in the world, subject to eligibility. From document preparation to the completed notarial act, our platform ensures a secure and convenient experience.

01

Confirm Your Document

Select the type of notarial act you need — from wills and trusts to passport certifications or powers of attorney. Our team reviews your document to ensure it meets all legal requirements before we proceed.

02

Prepare Identification

Have your valid government-issued photo ID ready. Our RON platform uses secure identity verification and knowledge-based authentication (KBA) to confirm your identity, ensuring compliance with notary public license law.

03

Join the RON Platform Session

Connect with a commissioned notary public via our trusted platforms — Notarize/Proof, BlueNotary, or CyberizeIt. The live audio-video session is recorded and conducted with the same legal authority as an in-person notarization.

04

Receive the Completed Notarial Act

Once the session is complete, your electronically signed and sealed document is delivered immediately. The tamper-evident digital certificate ensures your notarized document is recognized and legally binding.

What to Prepare Before Your Session

To ensure a smooth, efficient, and legally compliant remote online notarization, we recommend having the following items ready before your scheduled session. Proper preparation helps us serve you with the white-glove professionalism you deserve.

Valid Identification

Government-issued photo ID such as a driver's license, passport, or state ID card. Your identification will be verified through our secure platform's credential analysis technology.

Complete Unsigned Documents Where Appropriate

Have your documents prepared but unsigned where a notarial act is required. We will witness your electronic signature during the live session to ensure full legal validity.

Reliable Internet and Camera Access

A stable internet connection and a device with a functioning camera and microphone are essential for identity verification and the real-time audio-visual communication required by law.

Platform requirements may vary. Contact our team for guidance on your specific document type and notary platform.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common inquiries about our remote online notary services. If you have additional questions, we are here to help.

Can I use this service from outside the United States?

Absolutely. Our Remote Online Notary (RON) service is designed for US citizens anywhere in the world. Whether you are traveling, living abroad, or stationed overseas, you can complete your notarial acts online with a commissioned US notary public. All you need is a reliable internet connection and a valid US government-issued ID.

Which platforms are supported?

We operate on the industry's most trusted and secure platforms: Notarize/Proof, BlueNotary, and CyberizeIt. These platforms are fully compliant with state laws and provide a seamless, encrypted audio-visual session. Once you book your appointment, we will guide you to the appropriate platform and walk you through the simple setup process.

What kinds of documents can be notarized?

We offer a comprehensive range of notarial acts. This includes, but is not limited to, wills and trusts, powers of attorney, loan signings, passport certifications, affidavits, contracts, jurats, acknowledgements, and property or escrow documents. If you need a specific document notarized, such as a notary public power of attorney, please contact us to confirm the details and schedule your session.

Is 24-hour service guaranteed?

We strive to accommodate near-round-the-clock service based on availability. While we aim to fulfill 24-hour requests, some restrictions may apply depending on the complexity of the document and the platform's operating hours. We recommend calling or messaging us today to secure your preferred time slot, and we will do our absolute best to meet your timeline.

What if my document has special requirements?

Special requirements are our expertise. Whether you need a certified translation attached, multiple witnesses, or a specific notarial certificate format, we can adapt our service to meet your document's unique needs. We recommend reaching out to discuss the specifics before your session so we can ensure full compliance with the relevant legal notary standards and prepare the session accordingly.

Still have questions? We are ready to assist you with your legal notary needs.
